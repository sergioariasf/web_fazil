Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Information Collection

Our application connects to the Garmin Health API to retrieve physiological and activity data. Specific data collected includes:

  • Biological metrics (VO2Max, heart rate, power zones).
  • Activity history (cycling workouts, duration, intensity).
  • Basic profile information provided by Garmin.

2. Use of Information

The data is used exclusively for:

  • Generating metabolic and physiological diagnosis using deterministic models and Cognitive AI.
  • Dynamic training load planning and optimization.
  • Displaying performance metrics in the Fazil personal dashboard (Appsmith).

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases. We implement standard encryption and access control measures to ensure your information remains confidential and protected.

4. Garmin API Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with Garmin Health API usage requirements. Users may revoke application access at any time through their Garmin Connect settings or by contacting us directly.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy or your data, please contact Sergio Arias via LinkedIn.
